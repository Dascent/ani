I

The intention is strongly directed to I / me... has a stronger defined "target" and also appeals to both conscious and subconscious .

Roots of the symbol are obviously from I character small letter i (English I as me).
Sign Language has two versions , the index finger pointing and touching the chest or full hand, palm upwards, movement from above chest towards down (near belly area or below, in natural movement way).

These movements (defined as I, me) are not specific to Sign Language only, so the glyph is illustrating the hand position and the head to visually define an individual.

The purpose of this symbol is to enhance the vibration of the created sigil replacing the removed I vowel.
